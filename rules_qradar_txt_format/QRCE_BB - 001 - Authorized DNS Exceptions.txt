Apply QRCE:BB - 001 - Authorized DNS Exceptions on events or flows which are detected by the Local system
  and when the destination IP is one of the following 8.8.8.8, 8.8.4.4
  and when the source IP is one of the following 192.168.1.130

Note: This building block is designed to exclude devices with hard coded DNS servers (IoT). The source and destination IPs can be changed depending on your required exclusions in the environment.
